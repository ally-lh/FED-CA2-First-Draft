Values

Freedom

I place great importance on being allowed to have my personal thoughts and take on things, as well as freedom of speech. I hate being constrained by certain rules that stops me from being who I am or causes me to go against my beliefs.

Self-Respect

I understand my own self-worth and will never purposely put myself down infront of others or conform to peer pressure. I believe only having self-respect can you treat others with dignity and respect.

Happiness

I believe that what is most important is to live in the present, rather than worry for the future. I won't fret over what may happen in the future and just ensure that in the present, I am content with what I have. After all, you can't turn back time.

— The Debater: ENTP-T —

Quick-witted and audacious, Debaters aren’t afraid to disagree with the status quo. In fact, they’re not afraid to disagree with pretty much anything or anyone. Few things light up people with this personality type more than a bit of verbal sparring – and if the conversation veers into controversial terrain, so much the better. It would be a mistake, though, to think of Debaters as disagreeable or mean-spirited. Instead, people with this personality type are knowledgeable and curious, with a playful sense of humor, and they can be incredibly entertaining. They simply have an offbeat, contrarian idea of fun – one that involves a healthy dose of spirited debate.
